This game was originally planned for Android/mobile devices
The version in this zip file is a PC demo

Gameplay mechanics:
Players can only left click on white cubes
Grey cubes will change into either red,blue or white after certain time.
Red Cubes will only disapear when lined up horizontally in 3 or more groups.
Blue Cubes will only disapear when lined up vertically in 3 or more groups.
Game objective:
Prevent the cubes from stacking towards the top of the screen for as long as you can.

Notes:
Defeat conditions are still buggy and may or may not trigger properly.
